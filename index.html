Welcome to Palawan

✦

Palawan is an archipelagic province in the Philippines that is regularly hailed as the "Last Frontier" and the "Best Island in the World." From its crystal-clear waters to its towering limestone cliffs, Palawan offers an escape into a world of natural wonder and tranquility.
